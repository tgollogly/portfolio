Site information

Privacy & AI notice

This is a personal portfolio site. It is deliberately simple about data: it doesn’t track you, and it doesn’t want your personal information.

Cookies and tracking

This site sets no cookies. There is no analytics, no tag manager, no advertising pixel and no visitor tracking of any kind. Nothing is written to local storage. That is why you aren’t seeing a cookie banner — there is nothing to consent to.

What the demos do with what you type

Most of the demos run entirely in your browser and send nothing anywhere:

  • Beneish M-Score Screener — the figures you enter never leave your device.
  • BundleBuilder — case details stay in the page and are gone when you close the tab.
  • SQL Lab — the database runs in your browser; queries are not sent to a server.
  • Test Bench — code and tests execute locally.
  • BOM Desk — all data is invented sample data held in the page only.

The two features that do use AI

The AI assistant (the chat bubble) and the AI ATS Resume Matcher send what you type to Google’s Gemini API to generate a response. The request passes through a Cloudflare Worker that holds the API key, so the key is never exposed in your browser.

This site does not store, log or retain what you send. Once the response is generated it exists only in your browser window. However, the text is processed by Google under their own terms, so please don’t paste anything confidential — other people’s personal details, medical or financial information, or anything covered by an NDA. A CV and a job advert are fine; a client file is not.

AI-generated output can be wrong. Treat the match score, the cover letter and the CV rewrite as drafts to check, not as finished documents.

Third-party services

Loading a page here may involve requests to these services, which will see your IP address as a normal part of serving a request:

  • Cloudflare — hosting and the serverless backend
  • Google Fonts — typefaces
  • cdnjs and unpkg — JavaScript libraries used by some demos
  • Open-Meteo — weather and climate data (Heat Anomaly Detector, Thermal Compare)
  • RainViewer — precipitation radar; CARTO and AWS elevation tiles — map imagery (Heat Anomaly Detector)
  • Google Gemini — only for the two AI features described above

Not professional advice

Everything here is a portfolio demonstration. The Beneish screener is not financial or accounting advice; BundleBuilder is a document-organising tool and not legal advice; the Heat Anomaly Detector is indicative and not an official public-health warning; BOM Desk uses invented data and is not a deployment of any live system. Don’t rely on any of it for a real decision.
